Our Story

LIC Plumbing And Drain Cleaning was established with a simple mission: to provide reliable, high-quality plumbing services to the Long Island City community and surrounding areas. What began as a small operation has grown into a trusted plumbing company with a reputation for excellence.

Our founder began his career as an apprentice plumber, learning the trade from the ground up. After gaining years of experience working for various plumbing companies in New York, he decided to start his own business that would emphasize quality workmanship, honest pricing, and exceptional customer service.

Today, LIC Plumbing And Drain Cleaning is staffed by a team of highly skilled plumbers who share our commitment to excellence. We've built our reputation one satisfied customer at a time, and we're proud to be the plumbing company of choice for many residents and businesses in Queens, NY.

Throughout our growth, we've remained true to our core values: integrity, quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction. These principles guide every aspect of our business, from how we train our technicians to how we interact with our customers.

Our Journey
